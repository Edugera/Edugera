## About Me

I have worked in the Brazilian free energy market since 2009, with experience in commercialization, contract negotiation, portfolio construction, pricing, risk management and customer development.

Today, I am building **GridLedger**, an infrastructure initiative for auditable contract records, collateral coordination, risk controls and programmable settlement workflows for energy market agents.

I also teach Mathematics and create educational content connecting math, energy markets, finance, data and technology.

> Minha tese: mercados complexos precisam de clareza operacional, dados confiaveis e infraestrutura auditavel.
